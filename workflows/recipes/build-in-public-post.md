# Recipe: Build-in-Public Numbers Post

**Goal:** the highest-performing SaaS founder format of 2026 — one REAL number
+ the decision behind it, posted from the founder's personal profile.
Format: number → context → decision → falsifiable take.
**When to use:** weekly LinkedIn/X founder posts, "share our numbers", BIP updates.
**Prerequisites:** ⚠️ **The user supplies the numbers, every time.** Waitlist
count, MRR (including $0 — honesty IS the format), churned users, cohort
metrics. The agent NEVER estimates, extrapolates, or reuses stale numbers.
Standing weekly prompt: *"Numbers for this week's build-in-public post?"*

## The plan
1. **Intake:** this week's number(s) + what decision they drove, from the user.
2. **Write** (`/write-copy`): open with the naked number, no throat-clearing.
   Specific ("14 waitlist signups, 9 from one comment") beats round. End with a
   falsifiable take someone could disagree with — that's what LinkedIn 2026
   rewards. 2-3 posts/week beats daily filler.
3. **Optional stat card** (`renderSlideStill`, $0.07): the number huge in navy,
   gold accent only if it's a win, mist background from the style ref.
4. Post text goes out natively (no packaging needed for text); card attaches
   on LinkedIn.

## Budget
$0–0.07/post. The cheapest format in the kit — and the one that builds pipeline.

## Success signals
DMs and profile visits, not likes. A number that triggers questions becomes a
video essay or storytime. Three weeks of silence → the takes aren't falsifiable
enough; sharpen them.
