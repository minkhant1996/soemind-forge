# Recipe: "What If" Scenario

**Goal:** a 20–30s surreal counterfactual that IS the product philosophy —
"What if you could hear what investors really think?" → snap to reality →
"that's literally what the Investor Agent does." Top AI-native viral style;
uniquely on-brand for products whose feature-set is itself a what-if machine.
**When to use:** "what if video", product-philosophy content, promotional slots
that shouldn't feel like ads.
**Prerequisites:** preflight; the what-if must map honestly to a real product
capability — surreal staging, never a misleading claim.

## The plan
1. **Pick the counterfactual** from a product capability (one per piece).
   Structure: surreal setup (2 scenes) → SNAP to reality (hard cut) → one-line
   product tie → CTA.
2. **2–3 keyframes** (`generateImageVariation` on the style ref — surreal but
   inside the brand palette; the snap-to-reality frame is deliberately plain). ~$0.20
3. **Veo clips** for the surreal scenes (8s, fast); the reality beat can be a
   still. ~$1.60
4. **VO adaptive:** wonder/dreamlike for the setup, deadpan for the snap
   (`audioProfile` per segment, `pace: natural`). ~$0.01
5. `assembleStoryFilm` (logo end card) → package.

## Budget
$2–3/piece, biweekly.

## Success signals
Comments ANSWERING the what-if question — that's the engagement loop, and every
reply is a validation conversation starter. Reply to all of them.
