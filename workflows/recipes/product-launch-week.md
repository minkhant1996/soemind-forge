# Recipe: Product Launch Week

**Goal:** build anticipation, launch with proof, convert the interested.
**When to use:** a new product/flavor/feature ships on a known date.
**Prerequisites:** product asset registered (photos or generated mockup),
brand.md complete, an offer/CTA, launch date. Real social proof if any —
never invented.

## The plan (7 days, launch on Day 4)

Structure: tease → reveal → launch → proof → close. Adapt platforms to the
user's mix; counts below assume IG + TikTok as primary.

| Day | Piece | Skill | Why |
|-----|-------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Teaser image — cropped/mystery detail of the product, "something's coming" | `/generate-image` | Curiosity beats announcement; withhold the reveal |
| 2 | Behind-the-scenes video (8-15s) — making/testing the product | `/generate-video` | Authenticity primes trust before the sales ask |
| 3 | Countdown story + email/DM-list nudge | `/generate-image` (9:16) | Capture intent you can convert tomorrow |
| 4 | **LAUNCH:** hero video (15-30s) + feed image + full-offer caption | `/generate-video`, `/generate-image`, `/write-copy` | The main event — hook, product, offer, CTA in the first 3s |
| 5 | Proof piece — testimonial ad (real quotes only) or detailed feature close-up | [testimonial-ad.md](./testimonial-ad.md) or `/generate-image` | Day-after doubters need evidence, not repetition |
| 6 | Objection-handler carousel — top 3 hesitations, answered | `/generate-image` (carousel) | Converts the "interested but unsure" middle |
| 7 | Last-call story + recap reel of the week | `/generate-video` (or reuse clips) | Urgency close; deadline must be real |

## Execution notes

- Generate Days 1-4 up front (locked product + character refs keep them
  consistent); Days 5-7 benefit from waiting — real early reactions may
  replace planned content.
- One message per day. The offer appears Day 4-7 only; Days 1-3 never sell.
- A/B the Day 4 hook (`/preview-pick` Pattern D) — it's the highest-leverage
  creative of the week.
- Package everything per platform as you go (`/package-content`).

## Budget

~$8-20: hero video is the bulk (~$5-12 with storyboard previews); images
~$0.07 each; copy pennies. Estimate against the user's platform count and
video length before starting.

## Success signals

Day 1-3: saves, shares, replies ("what is it?"). Day 4: CTR to the offer.
Day 5-7: conversion. If Day 1-3 engagement is dead, rework the Day 4 hook
before launching — the teaser told you the angle isn't landing.
