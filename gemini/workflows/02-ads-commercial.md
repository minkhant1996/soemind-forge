# Ads & Commercial Videos

> High-impact promotional content optimized for attention and conversion.

---

## When to Use

- Product launches
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Social media ads
- App store previews
- Promotional trailers

**Ideal Duration**: 15-30 seconds (2-4 clips)

---

## Ad Structures

### The Hook-Problem-Solution (15s)

Best for: Social ads, quick conversions

```
HOOK (0-3s)
├── Attention grabber
├── Pattern interrupt
└── "Wait, what?"

PROBLEM (3-8s)
├── Pain point visualization
├── Relatable frustration
└── "This is me"

SOLUTION (8-15s)
├── Product reveal
├── Magic moment
└── Call to action
```

**Clip Breakdown**:
| Clip | Section | Duration | Purpose |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Hook + Problem | 8s | Grab attention, show pain |
| 2 | Solution | 7s | Product magic, CTA |

---

### The AIDA Formula (30s)

Best for: Full product story, brand building

```
ATTENTION (0-5s)
├── Visual hook
├── Unexpected moment
└── Stop the scroll

INTEREST (5-12s)
├── Problem revealed
├── Emotional connection
└── "Tell me more"

DESIRE (12-22s)
├── Solution shown
├── Benefits visualized
├── Aspirational outcome
└── "I want this"

ACTION (22-30s)
├── Clear CTA
├── Urgency element
└── Brand moment
```

**Clip Breakdown**:
| Clip | Section | Duration | Purpose |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Attention | 5s | Visual hook |
| 2 | Interest | 8s | Problem + emotion |
| 3 | Desire | 10s | Solution benefits |
| 4 | Action | 7s | CTA + brand |

---

### The Before-After-Bridge (20s)

Best for: Transformation products, testimonials

```
BEFORE (0-7s)
├── Life without product
├── Pain/frustration
└── Relatable struggle

AFTER (7-14s)
├── Life with product
├── Joy/success
└── Aspirational state

BRIDGE (14-20s)
├── Product as the bridge
├── How to get there
└── CTA
```

## Ad-Specific Prompt Elements

### Hook Shots (First 3 seconds)

**Pattern Interrupts**:
```
- Unexpected scale (tiny/huge)
- Impossible physics
- Color pop against neutral
- Direct eye contact
- Motion toward camera
- Something falling/breaking
```

**Hook Prompt Example**:
```
Dramatic macro shot of a glowing blue cursor blinking on a dark screen.
Suddenly, lines of colorful code begin cascading upward like a waterfall.
High contrast, electric blue and purple on pure black.
Camera rapidly pushes into the screen.
Cyberpunk aesthetic, ultra sharp, 4K commercial quality.
```

---

### Product Reveal Shots

**The Hero Shot**:
```
Cinematic product shot of [PRODUCT] floating in center frame.
Soft gradient background transitioning from [COLOR1] to [COLOR2].
Dramatic rim lighting creating a glowing halo effect.
Camera slowly orbits 45 degrees around the product.
Apple commercial aesthetic, pristine, premium quality.
```

**The In-Use Shot**:
```
Over-the-shoulder shot of [USER DESCRIPTION] using [PRODUCT].
Screen shows [INTERFACE DESCRIPTION] with [KEY FEATURE] highlighted.
Soft natural lighting, clean modern environment.
Shallow depth of field focusing on [KEY ELEMENT].
Lifestyle commercial quality, aspirational but relatable.
```

---

### Call-to-Action Shots

**The Brand Moment**:
```
Clean white/black background with [LOGO/PRODUCT] centered.
Subtle particle effects or light rays.
Text appears: "[CTA TEXT]"
Elegant fade or zoom transition.
Minimal, premium, Apple/Google aesthetic.
```

## Visual Techniques for Ads

### Speed and Energy

| Technique | Use When | Prompt Keywords |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| Speed ramp | Showing efficiency | "time-lapse", "accelerated motion" |
| Slow motion | Hero moment | "slow motion", "dramatic pause" |
| Quick cuts | Building energy | Generate shorter clips (5s) |
| Smooth flow | Premium feel | "fluid motion", "continuous take" |

### Color Psychology

| Color | Emotion | Use For |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Blue | Trust, tech | Software, B2B |
| Orange | Energy, CTA | Buttons, urgency |
| Green | Growth, success | Results, eco |
| Purple | Premium, creative | Luxury, design |
| Red | Urgency, passion | Sales, food |

```markdown

- Primary color: [Brand color]
```

## Example Prompts: SaaS Product Ad

**Clip 1: Hook**
```
Extreme close-up of an eye reflecting a screen full of overwhelming data.
Pupil dilates with stress. Red and orange warning colors reflect in the eye.
Dramatic shallow depth of field, cinematic tension.
Camera slowly pulls back.
Dark, moody, thriller aesthetic, commercial quality.
```

**Clip 2: Problem**
```
Wide shot of a stressed professional surrounded by floating screens and documents.
Papers flying chaotically, multiple browser tabs visible, notification sounds implied.
Harsh overhead lighting, cluttered desk, gray corporate environment.
Camera slowly rotates showing the chaos.
Overwhelming but relatable, commercial quality.
```

**Clip 3: Solution**
```
Same professional now calm, single clean screen showing organized dashboard.
Beautiful data visualization with blue and green colors.
Soft natural lighting, minimalist desk, plant in background.
Satisfied smile, relaxed posture.
Camera gently pushes in on the peaceful scene.
Aspirational, Apple aesthetic, premium commercial quality.
```

**Clip 4: CTA**
```
Clean white background with product logo centered.
Subtle blue particles floating upward like data streams.
Logo glows softly, tagline fades in below.
"Start your free trial"
Minimal, premium, tech commercial quality.
```

## Checklist

Before generating:

- [ ] Ad objective defined (awareness/conversion)
- [ ] Platform and aspect ratio chosen
- [ ] Hook concept strong (pattern interrupt)
- [ ] Problem clearly visualized
- [ ] Solution = magic moment
- [ ] CTA is clear and visible
- [ ] Brand colors consistent throughout
- [ ] Duration fits platform requirements
