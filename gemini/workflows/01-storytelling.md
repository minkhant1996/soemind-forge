# Storytelling & Narrative Videos

> Create emotionally engaging brand stories with consistent characters and cinematic quality.

---

## When to Use

- Brand origin stories
- Customer journey narratives
- Emotional product stories
- Mini-documentaries
- Inspirational content

**Ideal Duration**: 30-60 seconds (4-8 clips)

---

## Story Structures

### 3-Act Structure (Simplest)

Best for: Short ads, social content (15-30s)

```
ACT 1: SETUP (20%)
├── Introduce character
├── Show their world
└── Establish the problem

ACT 2: CONFRONTATION (60%)
├── Character struggles
├── Discovers solution
└── Begins transformation

ACT 3: RESOLUTION (20%)
├── Problem solved
├── New status quo
└── Call to action
```

**Example - 30 Second Story (4 clips)**:
| Clip | Act | Duration | Beat |
|------|-----|----------|------|
| 1 | Setup | 8s | Developer frustrated with bugs |
| 2 | Confrontation | 8s | Discovers AI coding assistant |
| 3 | Confrontation | 8s | Experiences the magic of AI help |
| 4 | Resolution | 6s | Confident, productive, smiling |

---

### 5-Act Structure (Classic)

Best for: Longer narratives, emotional depth (45-60s)

```
ACT 1: EXPOSITION
├── Introduce protagonist
├── Establish normal world
└── Hint at desire/need

ACT 2: RISING ACTION
├── Inciting incident
├── Character commits to journey
└── First obstacles appear

ACT 3: CLIMAX
├── Point of no return
├── Biggest challenge
└── Transformation moment

ACT 4: FALLING ACTION
├── Consequences unfold
├── New skills applied
└── Victory in sight

ACT 5: RESOLUTION
├── New equilibrium
├── Character transformed
└── Emotional payoff
```

**Example - 40 Second Story (5 clips)**:
| Clip | Act | Duration | Beat |
|------|-----|----------|------|
| 1 | Exposition | 8s | Developer overwhelmed by code |
| 2 | Rising Action | 8s | Discovers AI assistant, curious |
| 3 | Climax | 8s | First successful AI completion |
| 4 | Falling Action | 8s | In the flow, working harmoniously |
| 5 | Resolution | 8s | Triumphant, job done, satisfied |

---

### Hero's Journey (Abbreviated)

Best for: Aspirational brand stories, transformation narratives

```
ORDINARY WORLD → CALL TO ADVENTURE → TRANSFORMATION → RETURN WITH ELIXIR
```

## Visual Style Guide Template

**Copy and customize for your project:**

```markdown
## Visual Style Guide: [PROJECT NAME]

### Character
- Gender/Age: [e.g., Male, early 30s]
- Appearance: [e.g., Dark hair, light stubble, friendly face]
- Clothing: [e.g., Navy blue hoodie, simple watch]
- Expression range: [e.g., Frustrated → Curious → Confident → Triumphant]

### Setting
- Location: [e.g., Modern home office]
- Key props: [e.g., Ultrawide monitor, minimal desk, coffee mug]
- Background: [e.g., Bookshelf with plants, window with blinds]

### Color Palette
- Primary: [e.g., Deep navy blue #1a237e]
- Secondary: [e.g., Cyan accent #00bcd4]
- Accent: [e.g., Warm gold #ffa000]
- Mood shift: [e.g., Red/harsh → Blue/calm → Gold/warm]

### Lighting
- Key light: [e.g., Monitor glow, screen reflections]
- Fill light: [e.g., Soft ambient from room]
- Accent: [e.g., Golden hour through window]
- Progression: [e.g., Harsh → Soft → Warm]

### Camera
- Lens feel: [e.g., 50mm equivalent, shallow DOF]
- Movement: [e.g., Slow push-ins, gentle orbits]
- Angles: [e.g., Eye level, slightly low for hero shots]

### Style References
- [e.g., Apple product videos]
- [e.g., Google Pixel commercials]
- [e.g., Calm meditation app aesthetic]
```

## Prompt Formula for Narrative Clips

### The Master Formula

```
[SHOT TYPE], [CHARACTER DESCRIPTION doing ACTION].
[ENVIRONMENT DETAILS].
[LIGHTING DESCRIPTION] creating [MOOD].
[CAMERA MOVEMENT].
[STYLE KEYWORDS], [QUALITY KEYWORDS].
```

### Example Prompts by Story Beat

**Beat 1: The Struggle**
```
Cinematic medium close-up, a tired male developer in his early 30s with dark hair
wearing a navy blue hoodie, rubbing his eyes in frustration.
Sitting at a minimal desk with an ultrawide monitor showing red error messages.
Harsh monitor light casting blue shadows on his face, dark moody atmosphere.
Camera slowly pushes in on his exhausted expression.
Cinematic shallow depth of field, professional commercial quality, 4K.
```

**Beat 2: The Discovery**
```
Cinematic medium shot, same male developer in navy blue hoodie leaning forward
with curiosity and hope.
Ultrawide monitor now shows a clean AI assistant interface with cyan accents.
Soft cyan glow from screen illuminates his face, warm light beginning through window.
Camera slowly orbits from side profile toward front.
Consistent moody blue and cyan color grade, cinematic quality.
```

**Beat 3: The Transformation**
```
Cinematic close-up, same male developer in navy blue hoodie, eyes widening
with amazement, slight smile forming.
Monitor shows code auto-completing with beautiful syntax highlighting.
Balanced warm golden light and cool monitor glow, hopeful atmosphere.
Camera holds steady with subtle push in.
Cinematic shallow depth of field, commercial quality.
```

**Beat 4: The Triumph**
```
Cinematic medium wide shot, same male developer in navy blue hoodie leaning back
with satisfied smile, arms relaxed.
Monitor displays "Build Successful" with green checkmarks.
Golden hour light flooding the room, warm and triumphant atmosphere.
Camera slowly pulls back revealing the peaceful workspace.
Cinematic lens flare, aspirational commercial quality, 4K.
```

## Emotional Progression

Map emotions to visual elements:

| Emotion | Lighting | Colors | Camera |
|---------|----------|--------|--------|
| Frustration | Harsh, high contrast | Red, cold blue | Tight, static |
| Curiosity | Softening | Cyan appearing | Push in |
| Hope | Warming | Blue + gold | Gentle orbit |
| Joy | Bright, balanced | Warm gold | Pull back |
| Triumph | Golden, glowing | Gold dominant | Wide reveal |

---

## Audio Pairing

| Story Beat | Music Style | Voice Tone |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| Setup/Problem | Tense, minimal | Concerned, tired |
| Discovery | Building, hopeful | Curious, intrigued |
| Transformation | Swelling, emotional | Excited, amazed |
| Resolution | Triumphant, full | Confident, satisfied |

## Checklist

Before generating:

- [ ] Story structure chosen (3-act or 5-act)
- [ ] Visual style guide complete
- [ ] Character description locked (exact details)
- [ ] Color palette defined (with progression)
- [ ] Each clip's emotion mapped
- [ ] Prompts written with consistent character details
- [ ] Keyframe images generated (optional but recommended)
