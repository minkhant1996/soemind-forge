# Recipe: 30-Day Authority Calendar

**Goal:** become the account people follow for a topic — consistent, useful,
recognizable.
**When to use:** "build my audience", "post consistently", "become known for X".
**Prerequisites:** brand.md with a clear topic/niche, audience pain points in
project.md, a locked character (or explicit faceless style) for visual
consistency.

## The plan (30 days, pillar rotation)

Weekly rhythm — repeat 4×, escalating familiarity:

| Slot | Pillar | Piece | Skill |
|------|--------|-------|-------|
| Mon | Educational (40%) | How-to carousel or 30-60s explainer | `/generate-image` / `/generate-video` |
| Tue | Educational | Quick tip — single image + strong caption | `/generate-image` + `/write-copy` |
| Wed | Engagement (20%) | Question / hot take / this-or-that | `/write-copy` (text-first) |
| Thu | Educational | Myth-buster or mistake-to-avoid short | `/generate-video` |
| Fri | Behind-scenes (10%) | Process, workspace, lesson learned | `/generate-image` |
| Sat | Promotional (20%) | Soft pitch — result or use-case, CTA | `/generate-video` or carousel |
| Sun | Testimonial (10%) | Real proof (or curate community wins) | `/generate-image` — **real quotes only** |

Build with `/plan-content` into the standard calendar format, then execute
day-by-day with `/produce-content`.

## Execution notes

- **Batch weekly, not monthly:** produce week 1, post it, read what worked,
  then produce week 2 with that knowledge. The calendar is a hypothesis.
- Reuse one locked character/style ref in every visual — recognizability IS
  the product of this recipe.
- Weeks 3-4: double down on the two best-performing formats; drop the worst.
- Each educational piece answers ONE audience pain point from project.md.
  30 days ≈ your pain-point list × angles; if the list is thin, interview the
  user for more before planning.
- Repurpose aggressively: Monday's carousel becomes Thursday's short script
  (`/repurpose-content` thinking, even for generated content).

## Budget

~$15-40/month depending on video share: ~12 images (~$1), ~8 short videos
($1-4 each with preview discipline), copy negligible. Suggest a monthly cap
(`setBudgetCap`) matching this before starting.

## Success signals

Follows and saves per post trending up week-over-week; DMs/questions arriving
(authority signal); one format clearly outperforming by week 2 — that's the
account's signature format. No signal by week 3 = topic too broad; narrow the
niche, don't post harder.
